Instructions for filling up Form OPI

1) In the case of Mutual Funds, the details of Indian entity responsible for filing the Form OPI (i.e Promoter/ AMC of that Mutual Fund, as decided by the Mutual Fund), may be indicated in Section A (details of Indian entity), along with mentioning the name of the Mutual Fund.

2) Section C for Mutual Fund/ AIF/ VCF may be filled in by the Indian entity responsible to file the Form OPI (as designated by the fund).

3) The date may be mentioned in format DD/MM/YYYY format.

4) All amounts should be in actuals only.
